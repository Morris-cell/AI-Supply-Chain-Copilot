# Actual Delivery Date

## Definition

The actual date on which the customer received the shipment.

## Business Meaning

Used to determine whether the shipment was delivered on time.

## Possible Field Names

actual_delivery_date

delivery_date

received_date

shipment_delivery_date

actual_receipt_date

## Data Type

Date

## Related KPIs

- OTIF
- On-Time Delivery
- Delivery Lead Time
