# Promised Delivery Date

## Definition

The delivery date committed by the supplier when confirming the purchase order.

## Business Meaning

Represents the agreed delivery date used to evaluate supplier delivery performance.

## Possible Field Names

promised_delivery_date

confirmed_delivery_date

supplier_commit_date

expected_delivery_date

vendor_due_date

## Data Type

Date

## Related KPIs

- Supplier On-Time Delivery
- Supplier Lead Time
