# OTIF Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify the causes of poor delivery performance.

---

## Step 1

Calculate OTIF.

Questions:

* Is OTIF below target?
* Is OTIF declining?

---

## Step 2

Separate failures into two categories.

* Late Delivery
* Incomplete Delivery

---

## Step 3

Analyze Inventory Availability.

Questions:

* Were products available when ordered?
* Was safety stock sufficient?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Warehouse Operations.

Questions:

* Were picking or packing errors recorded?
* Was warehouse capacity sufficient?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Transportation.

Questions:

* Which carriers had the highest delay rate?
* Which delivery routes were problematic?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

* Improve inventory planning
* Optimize warehouse processes
* Review carrier performance
* Improve delivery scheduling
