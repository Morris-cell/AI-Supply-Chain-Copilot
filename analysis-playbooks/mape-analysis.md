# MAPE Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Evaluate the average forecast error and identify products or periods with poor forecast performance.

---

## Step 1

Calculate MAPE.

Questions:

- Is MAPE above target?
- Which products have the highest MAPE?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Product Segments.

Questions:

- Which product categories have poor forecast performance?
- Are slow-moving products affecting MAPE?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Demand Patterns.

Questions:

- Is demand highly seasonal?
- Were promotions or special events considered?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Forecast Process.

Questions:

- Are forecasting models appropriate?
- Are assumptions updated regularly?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Business Impact.

Questions:

- Did poor forecasting increase inventory?
- Did poor forecasting cause stock shortages?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Improve forecasting models
- Segment products by demand characteristics
- Increase forecast review frequency
- Improve collaboration across departments
