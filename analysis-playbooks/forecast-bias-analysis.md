# Forecast Bias Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify systematic forecasting tendencies and reduce consistent over-forecasting or under-forecasting.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Forecast Bias.

Questions:

- Is Forecast Bias consistently positive?
- Is Forecast Bias consistently negative?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Product Categories.

Questions:

- Which product categories are over-forecasted?
- Which product categories are under-forecasted?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Customer Demand.

Questions:

- Are customer demand patterns changing?
- Were promotional activities included?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Inventory Impact.

Questions:

- Did over-forecasting increase inventory?
- Did under-forecasting cause stockouts?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Forecast Process.

Questions:

- Are forecast assumptions reasonable?
- Are forecasts influenced by sales targets?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Improve forecasting assumptions
- Segment products by demand pattern
- Increase collaboration between Sales and Supply Chain
- Review forecasting process regularly
