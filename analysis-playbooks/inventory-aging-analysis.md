# Inventory Aging Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify inventory aging risks and determine the causes of slow-moving or obsolete inventory.

---

## Step 1

Calculate inventory age for each SKU.

Questions:

* How many days has each item remained in inventory?

---

## Step 2

Group inventory into aging buckets.

Questions:

* What percentage of inventory is older than 90 days?
* What percentage is older than 180 days?

---

## Step 3

Identify high-value aged inventory.

Questions:

* Which SKUs contribute most to aged inventory value?
* Which warehouses contain the highest aged inventory?

---

## Step 4

Analyze sales activity.

Questions:

* Has the product sold recently?
* Is demand declining?

---

## Step 5

Review replenishment policy.

Questions:

* Was excess inventory purchased?
* Is safety stock appropriate?

---

## Step 6

Generate recommendations.

Possible Actions:

* Liquidate obsolete inventory
* Reduce replenishment quantities
* Review product lifecycle
* Improve forecasting
