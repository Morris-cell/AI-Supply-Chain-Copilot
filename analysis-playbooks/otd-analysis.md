# OTD Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify the causes of late deliveries and improve delivery performance.

---

## Step 1

Calculate OTD.

Questions:

- Is OTD below target?
- Is OTD declining?

---

## Step 2

Identify delayed deliveries.

Questions:

- Which customers experienced delays?
- Which products were delayed?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Carrier Performance.

Questions:

- Which carriers have the highest late delivery rate?
- Are delays concentrated with specific carriers?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Warehouse Operations.

Questions:

- Were shipments released on time?
- Were picking or packing delays recorded?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Transportation Routes.

Questions:

- Which routes experience frequent delays?
- Are delays seasonal or location-specific?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Optimize carrier selection
- Improve warehouse dispatch process
- Adjust transportation schedules
- Increase shipment visibility
