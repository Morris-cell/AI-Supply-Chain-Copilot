# Supplier On-Time Delivery Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify the causes of supplier delivery delays and evaluate supplier delivery reliability.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Supplier OTD.

Questions:

- Is Supplier OTD below target?
- Which suppliers have the lowest OTD?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Delayed Purchase Orders.

Questions:

- Which purchase orders arrived late?
- Which materials were affected?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Supplier Performance.

Questions:

- Are delays concentrated among specific suppliers?
- Is supplier performance deteriorating?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Transportation.

Questions:

- Were delays caused by transportation?
- Were customs or import processes involved?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Inventory Impact.

Questions:

- Did late deliveries cause stock shortages?
- Was production affected?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Strengthen supplier performance monitoring
- Develop alternative suppliers
- Improve transportation planning
- Increase safety stock for critical materials
