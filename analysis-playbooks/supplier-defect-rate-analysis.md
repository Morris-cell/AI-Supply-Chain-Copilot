# Supplier Defect Rate Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify quality issues caused by suppliers and reduce incoming material defects.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Supplier Defect Rate.

Questions:

- Is Supplier Defect Rate above target?
- Which suppliers have the highest defect rates?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Defect Categories.

Questions:

- What types of defects occur most frequently?
- Are defects cosmetic or functional?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Supplier Performance.

Questions:

- Are defects concentrated in a small number of suppliers?
- Has supplier quality deteriorated over time?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Material Impact.

Questions:

- Which materials have the highest defect rate?
- Are critical materials affected?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Production Impact.

Questions:

- Did defects cause production delays?
- Did defects increase scrap or rework?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Perform supplier quality audits
- Require supplier corrective action plans (CAPA)
- Tighten incoming inspection
- Develop alternate suppliers
- Increase supplier quality monitoring
