# Fill Rate Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify the causes of inventory shortages and improve customer order fulfillment.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Fill Rate.

Questions:

- Is Fill Rate below target?
- Which products have the lowest Fill Rate?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Inventory Availability.

Questions:

- Are stockouts occurring?
- Which warehouses have insufficient inventory?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Supplier Performance.

Questions:

- Are supplier deliveries delayed?
- Is Supplier OTD declining?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Demand Forecast.

Questions:

- Is Forecast Accuracy decreasing?
- Was demand underestimated?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Replenishment Policy.

Questions:

- Is safety stock sufficient?
- Are reorder points appropriate?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Increase safety stock
- Improve forecast accuracy
- Reduce supplier lead time
- Improve replenishment policy
- Monitor supplier delivery performance
