# Supplier Lead Time Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify causes of supplier lead time variation and procurement delays.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Supplier Lead Time.

Questions:

- Is Lead Time increasing?
- Which suppliers exceed the target?

---

## Step 2

Compare Suppliers.

Questions:

- Which supplier has the longest lead time?
- Which supplier shows the highest variability?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Material Categories.

Questions:

- Which materials experience the longest lead time?
- Are critical materials affected?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Transportation.

Questions:

- Are delays caused by logistics?
- Are customs or import processes contributing?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Procurement Planning.

Questions:

- Are purchase orders placed too late?
- Is demand planning accurate?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Improve supplier collaboration
- Diversify suppliers
- Increase safety stock for critical materials
- Improve procurement planning
