# DIO Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify causes of excessive inventory holding time.

---

## Step 1

Calculate DIO.

Questions:

* Is DIO increasing?
* Is DIO above benchmark?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Inventory Aging.

Questions:

* How much inventory is older than 90 days?
* How much inventory is older than 180 days?

---

## Step 3

Identify Slow Moving SKUs.

Questions:

* Which SKUs contribute most to inventory value?
* Which SKUs have low sales velocity?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Forecast Accuracy.

Questions:

* Is forecast bias positive?
* Is over-forecasting occurring?

---

## Step 5

Review Replenishment Policy.

Questions:

* Is safety stock too high?
* Are reorder quantities excessive?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

* Reduce safety stock
* Optimize reorder points
* Liquidate obsolete inventory
* Improve forecast accuracy
