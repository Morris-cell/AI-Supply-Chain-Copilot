# Delivery Lead Time Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify bottlenecks in the order fulfillment process and reduce delivery lead time.

---

## Step 1

Calculate average Delivery Lead Time.

Questions:

- Is Delivery Lead Time increasing?
- Does it exceed the company target?

---

## Step 2

Analyze by customer.

Questions:

- Which customers experience the longest lead time?
- Are strategic customers affected?

---

## Step 3

Analyze by warehouse.

Questions:

- Which warehouse has the slowest fulfillment?
- Are picking or packing delays occurring?

---

## Step 4

Analyze by carrier.

Questions:

- Which carriers have the longest transportation time?
- Are delays concentrated with specific carriers?

---

## Step 5

Analyze transportation mode.

Questions:

- Is lead time different for air, sea, or road transportation?
- Are expedited shipments meeting expectations?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Improve warehouse operations
- Optimize transportation planning
- Improve carrier selection
- Reduce order processing time
- Improve inventory allocation
