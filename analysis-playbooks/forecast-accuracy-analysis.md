# Forecast Accuracy Analysis Playbook

## Objective

Identify the causes of inaccurate demand forecasts and improve planning performance.

---

## Step 1

Calculate Forecast Accuracy.

Questions:

- Is Forecast Accuracy below target?
- Which products have the lowest Forecast Accuracy?

---

## Step 2

Analyze Product Categories.

Questions:

- Which product categories have poor forecast performance?
- Are seasonal products affected?

---

## Step 3

Analyze Customer Demand.

Questions:

- Are customer buying patterns changing?
- Were promotions considered?

---

## Step 4

Analyze Historical Trends.

Questions:

- Does historical demand support the forecast?
- Are there unusual demand spikes?

---

## Step 5

Analyze Business Impact.

Questions:

- Did poor forecasts increase inventory?
- Did poor forecasts cause stockouts?

---

## Step 6

Generate Recommendations.

Possible Actions:

- Improve forecasting models
- Review promotional planning
- Increase collaboration between Sales and Supply Chain
- Update forecasting assumptions
