# High Supplier Defect Rate Due to Process Instability

## Background

Supplier Defect Rate increased from 0.8% to 4.5% over six weeks.

## Investigation

Most defective materials originated from one supplier after a manufacturing process change.

Incoming inspection identified dimensional defects exceeding specification limits.

## Impact

Production experienced increased rework.

Scrap costs increased by 18%.

Customer shipments were delayed due to replacement material shortages.

## Root Cause

Supplier manufacturing process instability.

## Recommendation

Conduct supplier quality audit.

Require corrective and preventive action (CAPA).

Increase inspection frequency until process stability is restored.

## Lessons Learned

Supplier quality issues can significantly impact production efficiency, inventory availability, and customer delivery performance.
