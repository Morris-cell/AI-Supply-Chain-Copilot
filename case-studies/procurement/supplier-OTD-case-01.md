# Low Supplier OTD Due to Transportation Delays

## Background

Supplier OTD declined from 97% to 90% over one quarter.

## Investigation

Most delayed purchase orders were imported through a congested shipping route.

Supplier production completed on schedule.

## Impact

Several production orders were postponed.

Inventory shortages reduced Fill Rate.

OTIF declined from 96% to 91%.

## Root Cause

Transportation delays caused late material arrivals.

## Recommendation

Review logistics providers.

Develop alternative shipping routes.

Increase safety stock for imported materials.

## Lessons Learned

Supplier delivery performance depends not only on supplier operations but also on transportation reliability.
