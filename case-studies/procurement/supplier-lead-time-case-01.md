# Long Supplier Lead Time Due to Supplier Capacity Constraints

## Background

Average Supplier Lead Time increased from 18 days to 31 days over two months.

## Investigation

A key supplier experienced production capacity shortages due to increased market demand.

Purchase order processing remained stable.

Transportation performance met expectations.

## Impact

Material shortages delayed production.

Inventory availability decreased.

OTIF dropped from 95% to 89%.

## Root Cause

Supplier production capacity constraints.

## Recommendation

Qualify additional suppliers.

Increase safety stock for critical materials.

Collaborate with suppliers on capacity planning.

## Lessons Learned

Supplier Lead Time directly affects inventory availability and customer delivery performance.
