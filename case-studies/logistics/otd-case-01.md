# Low OTD Caused by Carrier Performance

## Background

OTD decreased from 95% to 89% during the holiday season.

## Investigation

Late deliveries were concentrated in shipments handled by a single carrier.

Warehouse dispatch times remained within target.

## Impact

Customer complaints increased by 28%.

Several priority orders missed their promised delivery dates.

## Root Cause

Carrier capacity constraints during peak season caused transportation delays.

## Recommendation

Diversify carrier allocation.

Establish backup transportation providers.

Monitor carrier performance on a weekly basis.

## Lessons Learned

Delivery performance depends not only on internal operations but also on the reliability and capacity of logistics partners.
