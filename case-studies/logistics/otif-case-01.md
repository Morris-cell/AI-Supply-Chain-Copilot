# Low OTIF Caused by Inventory Shortage

## Background

OTIF decreased from 96% to 88% during Q2.

## Investigation

68% of failed orders were delivered late because inventory was unavailable at the requested shipping date.

Fill Rate also declined from 98% to 91%.

## Impact

Customer complaints increased by 35%.

Several key customers experienced delayed deliveries.

## Root Cause

Inventory shortages caused incomplete and delayed shipments.

## Recommendation

Review safety stock policy.

Improve demand forecasting.

Increase visibility of inventory availability.

## Lessons Learned

High OTIF depends not only on transportation performance but also on effective inventory management.
