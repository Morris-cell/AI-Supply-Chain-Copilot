# Increased Delivery Lead Time Due to Warehouse Bottlenecks

## Background

Average Delivery Lead Time increased from 4.2 days to 6.8 days during the previous quarter.

## Investigation

Warehouse processing time increased significantly because of labor shortages.

Transportation performance remained stable.

## Impact

Customer satisfaction scores declined.

More orders missed the promised delivery date.

## Root Cause

Warehouse picking and packing delays extended the overall fulfillment cycle.

## Recommendation

Increase warehouse staffing during peak periods.

Optimize picking routes.

Implement warehouse performance monitoring.

## Lessons Learned

Delivery delays are not always caused by transportation. Warehouse operations can be a significant contributor to longer lead times.
