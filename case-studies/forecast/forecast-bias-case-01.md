# Positive Forecast Bias Caused by Sales Target Inflation

## Background

Forecast Bias remained above +18% for four consecutive months.

## Investigation

Sales teams consistently forecasted higher demand to secure additional inventory before peak season.

Actual demand remained stable.

## Impact

Inventory Turnover decreased.

DIO increased from 42 days to 61 days.

Inventory Aging increased significantly.

## Root Cause

Sales forecasts were intentionally inflated.

## Recommendation

Separate sales targets from operational forecasts.

Introduce collaborative demand planning.

Monitor Forecast Bias monthly.

## Lessons Learned

Even with acceptable Forecast Accuracy, persistent Forecast Bias can create significant inventory and working capital problems.
