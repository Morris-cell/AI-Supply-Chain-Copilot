# Low Forecast Accuracy Due to Promotion Planning

## Background

Forecast Accuracy declined from 91% to 76% during a major promotional campaign.

## Investigation

The demand planning team did not incorporate the marketing promotion into the forecast.

Actual demand exceeded forecast by 38%.

## Impact

Inventory shortages occurred.

Supplier emergency orders increased.

Fill Rate declined from 98% to 90%.

OTIF decreased from 96% to 89%.

## Root Cause

Promotion activities were not integrated into the forecasting process.

## Recommendation

Integrate promotion planning into demand forecasting.

Strengthen collaboration between Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain.

## Lessons Learned

Accurate demand forecasting requires cross-functional collaboration, not only statistical models.
