# High MAPE Caused by Seasonal Demand

## Background

MAPE increased from 12% to 34% during the year-end holiday season.

## Investigation

Demand for seasonal products increased sharply.

Forecast models relied mainly on historical averages and did not adequately account for holiday effects.

## Impact

Several products experienced stockouts.

Emergency purchase orders increased.

Fill Rate decreased from 97% to 91%.

OTIF dropped from 95% to 90%.

## Root Cause

Seasonal demand patterns were not sufficiently incorporated into forecasting.

## Recommendation

Adopt seasonal forecasting models.

Incorporate holiday calendars into the forecasting process.

Review seasonal forecasts before peak periods.

## Lessons Learned

Forecasting methods should match demand characteristics. A single forecasting model may not perform well across all products.
