# High Inventory Aging Due to Product Obsolescence

## Background

The percentage of inventory older than 180 days increased from 8% to 24%.

## Investigation

Several discontinued products remained in stock.

No inventory liquidation process was in place.

## Impact

Inventory carrying costs increased.

Warehouse utilization exceeded 90%.

Risk of inventory write-offs increased.

## Root Cause

Obsolete inventory accumulated due to ineffective product lifecycle management.

## Recommendation

Dispose of obsolete inventory.

Strengthen inventory aging monitoring.

Establish regular inventory review meetings.

## Lessons Learned

Inventory aging should be monitored continuously to prevent long-term capital tied up in obsolete stock.
