# High DIO Caused by Slow-Moving Inventory

## Background

DIO increased from 52 days to 86 days.

## Investigation

35% of inventory value came from products with no sales in the last 120 days.

## Impact

Inventory carrying cost increased.

Warehouse utilization exceeded 90%.

## Root Cause

Slow-moving and obsolete inventory.

## Recommendation

Clear excess inventory.

Review product lifecycle management.

Reduce replenishment quantities.

## Lessons Learned

A small number of SKUs can contribute disproportionately to high DIO.
