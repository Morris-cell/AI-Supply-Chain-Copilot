# Inventory Turnover Decline Due to Excess Purchasing

## Background

Inventory Turnover decreased from 6.8 to 4.3.

## Investigation

Purchase quantities increased by 45%.

Sales volume remained stable.

Large orders were placed to obtain supplier discounts.

## Impact

Inventory value increased substantially.

Working capital requirements increased.

## Root Cause

Procurement decisions prioritized unit cost savings over inventory efficiency.

## Recommendation

Review Economic Order Quantity (EOQ).

Evaluate total inventory carrying cost.

Balance purchasing discounts against working capital impact.

## Lessons Learned

Lower purchase prices do not necessarily reduce overall supply chain costs.
