# Inventory Turnover Decline Due to Obsolete Inventory

## Background

Inventory Turnover decreased from 6.5 to 3.9.

## Investigation

22% of inventory value consisted of products with no sales activity for more than 180 days.

Several discontinued products remained in stock.

## Impact

Warehouse utilization exceeded 90%.

Inventory write-off risk increased.

## Root Cause

Obsolete and slow-moving inventory accumulated over time.

## Recommendation

Implement inventory aging reviews.

Liquidate obsolete inventory.

Strengthen product lifecycle management.

## Lessons Learned

A relatively small amount of obsolete inventory can significantly distort overall inventory performance metrics.
