# Low Fill Rate Due to Supplier Delays

## Background

Fill Rate declined from 98% to 90% over two months.

## Investigation

Supplier On-Time Delivery dropped from 96% to 85%.

Critical raw materials arrived later than planned.

Inventory availability decreased significantly.

## Impact

Customer backorders increased.

Lost sales increased.

OTIF decreased from 95% to 89%.

## Root Cause

Late supplier deliveries caused inventory shortages.

## Recommendation

Develop alternative suppliers.

Increase safety stock for critical materials.

Monitor supplier delivery performance weekly.

## Lessons Learned

High Fill Rate depends on reliable supplier performance and effective inventory planning.
