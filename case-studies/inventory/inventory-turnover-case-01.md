# Inventory Turnover Decline Due to Forecast Error

## Background

Inventory Turnover decreased from 7.2 to 4.8 within six months.

## Investigation

Forecast Accuracy dropped from 87% to 63%.

Demand forecasts consistently exceeded actual sales.

## Impact

Inventory value increased by 32%.

Inventory carrying costs increased significantly.

## Root Cause

Demand over-forecasting resulted in excessive inventory purchases.

## Recommendation

Improve forecasting methodology.

Implement forecast bias monitoring.

Align purchasing plans with actual demand trends.

## Lessons Learned

Inventory accumulation can occur even when procurement and warehouse operations are functioning normally if demand forecasts are inaccurate.
