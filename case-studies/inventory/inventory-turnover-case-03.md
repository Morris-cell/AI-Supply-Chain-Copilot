# Inventory Turnover Decline Due to Demand Decline

## Background

Inventory Turnover fell from 8.1 to 5.1 over one year.

## Investigation

Sales volume decreased by 28%.

Inventory purchasing levels remained unchanged.

Market demand weakened due to increased competition.

## Impact

Inventory aged significantly.

Cash conversion cycle increased.

## Root Cause

Demand reduction caused inventory to accumulate.

## Recommendation

Adjust demand forecasts.

Reduce replenishment frequency.

Review product portfolio strategy.

## Lessons Learned

Inventory performance can deteriorate rapidly when replenishment plans are not adjusted to changing market conditions.
